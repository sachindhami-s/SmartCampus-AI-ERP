# SmartCampus AI ERP

## Project Vision

SmartCampus AI ERP is an AI-powered College Management System designed to simplify academic and administrative operations.

The system provides a centralized platform where Students, Faculty, and Administrators can efficiently manage attendance, assignments, notes, results, timetables, notifications, and academic records.

The platform also integrates Artificial Intelligence to enhance learning by providing features such as:

- AI Study Planner
- AI Chatbot
- AI PDF Summarizer
- AI Quiz Generator
- AI Doubt Solver

The objective of this project is to reduce manual work, improve communication between all stakeholders, and provide a modern digital campus experience.

---

## Project Objectives

- Digitalize college management.
- Reduce paperwork.
- Improve communication.
- Provide role-based access.
- Automate repetitive tasks.
- Integrate Artificial Intelligence into education.
- Create a scalable and secure ERP system.
